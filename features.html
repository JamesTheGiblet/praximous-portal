Our Core Principles

Data Ownership

You control your data. Our tools are built on principles of data minimization and local-first storage. No tracking, no selling.

Decentralization

We leverage decentralized technologies to reduce reliance on single points of failure and control. Your identity, your keys.

Open Source

All our tools are open-source. We believe in transparency and community collaboration to build trustworthy software.

Privacy by Design

Privacy isn't an afterthought; it's a core feature. We use end-to-end encryption and privacy-preserving techniques.

Interoperability

Our tools are designed to work together and with other open standards, avoiding vendor lock-in and promoting a healthy ecosystem.

User Empowerment

We provide you with the tools and knowledge to navigate the digital world securely and independently.
