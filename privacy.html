Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We believe in transparency and data minimization.

It is The Digital Sovereignty Project's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website. The tools provided, like the README generator, operate entirely on your local machine. No data from the forms is sent to our servers.

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you (which, for our current tools, we don't). We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

We don’t share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

Our website may link to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.
